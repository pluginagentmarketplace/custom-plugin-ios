## Bottom Line Recommendations

### Best for Beginners:
1. **QA Engineer** - Easiest entry, good pay, path to development
2. **Flutter Developer** - Modern tech, high demand, cross-platform
3. **UX Designer** - Creative, bootcamp viable, growing field

### Best for Career Changers:
1. **Flutter Developer** - Fresh tech, no legacy baggage needed
2. **Product Manager** - Leverage previous industry experience
3. **QA Engineer** - Technical but approachable

### Best for Freelancers:
1. **Flutter Developer** - High demand for MVPs and startups
2. **iOS Developer** - Premium rates, dedicated market
3. **UX Designer** - Project-based work common

### Best for Long-term Salary:
1. **Product Manager** - Highest ceiling ($250k+)
2. **Server-Side Game Dev** - Specialized, high value ($220k+)
3. **iOS Developer** - Strong and stable ($210k+)
