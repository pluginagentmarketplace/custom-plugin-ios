## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge 1: "Math is too intimidating"
**Reality**: You don't need to be a math genius
**Solution**:
- Learn intuition first, formulas second
- Implement before memorizing
- Use visual explanations (3Blue1Brown)
- Many successful engineers aren't pure mathematicians
- **Key**: Understanding > memorization

### Challenge 2: "Too much to learn"
**Reality**: Field is huge, you can't learn everything
**Solution**:
- Follow structured paths (avoid random learning)
- Master 80% of concepts first
- Specialize after fundamentals
- One area at a time
- **Key**: Depth > breadth initially

### Challenge 3: "How do I stay current?"
**Reality**: Field changes every 3-6 months
**Solution**:
- Follow 5-10 key researchers on Twitter
- Read 1-2 arxiv papers per week
- Build projects with new techniques
- Participate in communities
- **Key**: Continuous learning is built-in

### Challenge 4: "Model doesn't improve"
**Reality**: ML is empirical, lots of experimentation
**Solution**:
- Better data > better models
- Try simple things first
- Use pretrained models
- Implement others' work first
- **Key**: Iteration > perfection
