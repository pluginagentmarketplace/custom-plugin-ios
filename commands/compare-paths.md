## Decision Framework

### Ask Yourself:

1. **Passion**: Which genuinely interests you?
   - Don't ignore this; you'll spend 10,000+ hours

2. **Market**: What's in high demand?
   - Job security and salary potential

3. **Timeline**: How quickly do you need income?
   - Some paths are faster than others

4. **Skills**: What do you already know?
   - Leverage existing skills to accelerate

5. **Career**: Where do you want to be in 5 years?
   - Different paths lead to different destinations
